Who we are.

We are NYC’s largest student-run organization of hackers, designers & builders. We host events that unite students and help them make stuff. Take a look at our calendar for past and future events.

Find out more.

  • Get Started with the Tech Scene

    Freshman Circuit

    Attend tech@nyu events and tour NYC startups with a small community of other freshmen. You'll get to meet startup employees to learn what they’re up to and where you might fit in. We’ve previously toured Kickstarter, eBay, The New York Times, Foursquare, Microsoft, Betaworks, and others.
