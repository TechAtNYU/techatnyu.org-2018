About tech@NYU

We are NYC’s largest student-run organization of hackers, designers and builders. We host events that unite students and help them make stuff.

We want students continually building anything together. Why? It takes a lot of junk, a bag of bad ideas and shoddy execution scars to learn and internalize the nuances of great product and business. Tech@NYU is the sandbox that students need: a place to experiment, a forum for recognition, a classroom for discussion and learning.
